  • Chloé

    Role : developer back/ additional content creator

    Time : 14 days

    Technos : Socket.io/MongoDB(not use at the final version)/RaspberryPi/Adobe premiere/Adobe photoshop

    The first project in my years at HETIC who will be continued if the jury see a real potential into this. There was some themes predefined,we choose 'Masterpieces of humanity'.

    After some reflexions/brainstorming, we concentrate our attention in one fact: Currently in France, there is not enough attention to the art in the young population. After asking people in the street,we confirmed this fact. there is 8/10 who can't answer correctly at the question : "Can you tell me the name of the 7 wonders of the world ?"

    To answer at this issue, we decided to create Chloé : a light night connected with an application.Parents who want to tell a story to cradle theirs childrens can just use their tablets.Chloe application contain stories about Masterpieces of humanity, in our exemple we selected the stories of Ghyzees's pyramid.If parents have bought the night light, they can diffuse additional content when they tell the stories to transfer childrens into the atmosphere of the stories. The additional content will be projected into the wall, it can be images/words or short videos.
